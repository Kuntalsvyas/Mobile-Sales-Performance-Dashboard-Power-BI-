## ğŸ“Œ Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive **Power BI Dashboard** designed to analyze and visualize mobile phone sales data. By transforming raw transactional records into an interactive visual report, I have provided a way for stakeholders to track sales trends, brand performance, and customer demographics in real-time.

The goal was to identify which mobile brands are driving revenue, how different payment methods are utilized, and which cities are the primary hubs for sales.

## ğŸ“Š Key Insights & KPIs
* **Revenue Analysis:** Tracking total sales value and units sold across different brands (Apple, Samsung, Vivo, Xiaomi, OnePlus).

* **Customer Demographics:** Visualizing sales distribution by **Customer Age** and **City** to identify target markets.
* **Payment Preferences:** Breakdowns of transactions via **UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, and Cash** to understand consumer behavior.
* **Rating Trends:** Correlating **Customer Ratings** with specific mobile models to identify top-tier products.
